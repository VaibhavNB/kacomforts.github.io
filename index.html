Tirupati

Kerala

Destinations

Hampi

Hampi || The heritage of karnataka

Explore Hampi, the awe-inspiring capital of the Vijayanagar Empire. Immerse yourself in a surreal landscape adorned with ancient ruins, intricate temples, and captivating history. Unearth the cultural richness of this UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Bengalore

Bengalore || Silicon city of India

"Bengaluru, the vibrant capital of Karnataka, seamlessly blends tradition and modernity. Renowned for its IT hub, lush gardens, and diverse culture, this dynamic city captivates with its rich heritage and progressive spirit."

Goa

Goa || The sea facing state

"Discover the sun-soaked beaches, vibrant nightlife, and rich cultural tapestry of Goa. Embrace the laid-back charm, from historic churches to bustling markets. Immerse yourself in a tropical paradise where every moment is a celebration."

Mysore

Mysore || Land of Palaces

Discover the cultural tapestry of Mysore, Karnataka. Home to the majestic Mysore Palace, vibrant markets, and the aromatic Mysore Pak, this city seamlessly blends tradition with modernity, inviting you to delve into its rich heritage and warm hospitality.

Bruhadeshwar Temple

Bruhadeshwar Tanjavur

Brihadeshwara Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage site in Thanjavur, India, dating back to the 11th century, is a marvel of Chola architecture. Its grandeur is epitomized by a towering vimana, intricate sculptures, and cultural significance.

Kanyakumari

Kanyakumari || The feet of India

"Discover Kanyakumari's enchanting beauty where three seas converge, offering breathtaking sunsets by the Vivekananda Rock Memorial. Immerse in cultural richness at the southernmost tip of India, where history meets serenity."
